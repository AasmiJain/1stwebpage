Wheat

Pigeon pea grows well in well-drained loamy soil with a pH of 6.5 to 7.5. It prefers a warm climate with temperatures between 26°C and 30°C. The crop is drought-tolerant and requires moderate rainfall.

Rice

Red lentil grows well in loamy soil with good drainage and a pH of 6.0 to 7.5. It prefers a cool climate with temperatures between 18°C and 25°C. Low to moderate rainfall and bright sunlight are ideal.

Maize

Green gram grows best in well-drained sandy loam soil with a pH of 6.0 to 7.0. It thrives in warm climates with temperatures between 25°C and 35°C. The crop requires moderate rainfall and full sunlight for good yield.

Oats
×

Oats

Moth bean grows well in sandy to loamy soils with good drainage and a pH of 6.0 to 7.5. It thrives in hot and dry climates with temperatures between 25°C and 35°C. The crop is highly drought-tolerant and requires low rainfall. It needs full sunlight for healthy growth.

Barley

Barley

Black gram grows best in fertile, well-drained loamy soil with a pH of 6.5 to 7.5. It requires a warm climate with temperatures between 25°C and 35°C and moderate rainfall. The crop needs full sunlight.

Sorghum/Jowar
×

Jowar

Chickpea grows well in well-drained sandy loam to loamy soil with a pH of 6.0 to 7.5. It prefers a cool and dry climate with temperatures between 20°C and 25°C. The crop requires moderate rainfall and plenty of sunlight.

Bajra

Lobia grows well in well-drained sandy loam to loamy soil with a pH of 5.5 to 7.5. It prefers a warm climate with temperatures between 20°C and 30°C. The crop is drought-tolerant, requires low to moderate rainfall, and grows best in full sunlight.

Quinoa

Quinoa

Field pea grows well in fertile, well-drained soil with a pH of 6.0 to 7.5. It thrives in cool climates with temperatures between 10°C and 25°C. The crop requires moderate moisture and sunlight.

Kuttu

Rajma grows best in well-drained loamy soil with a pH of 6.0 to 7.0. It prefers a cool to moderate climate with temperatures between 15°C and 25°C. The crop requires moderate rainfall and cannot tolerate waterlogging. Full sunlight is ideal for healthy growth and good yield.
