🌾 Crop Information

Rice

Requires high rainfall and warm climate.

Wheat

Grows best in cool climate.

Maize

Needs warm weather and good drainage.

Cotton

Best grown in black soil and high temperature.

🌦️ Weather Conditions

Weather plays a major role in crop growth. Temperature, humidity, and rainfall must be balanced.

  • Soil fertility
  • Water availability
  • Climate conditions
  • Farming techniques

🚜 Modern Farming

Use of AI and technology helps farmers improve crop production and reduce loss.

Choosing the right crop based on environment increases profit and reduces risk.
