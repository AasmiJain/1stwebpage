Mango

Mango grows best in well-drained loamy soil with a pH of 5.5 to 7.5. It requires a warm climate with temperatures between 24°C and 30°C and does not tolerate frost. Moderate humidity is suitable, and the plant needs full sunlight for healthy growth.

Apple

Apple grows well in well-drained loamy soil with a pH of 6.0 to 7.0. It requires a cold climate with temperatures between 10°C and 24°C. Chilling hours are important for fruiting. The plant needs full sunlight and moderate rainfall for proper growth.

Banana

Banana grows best in well-drained loamy soil rich in organic matter. It requires a warm and humid climate with temperatures between 25°C and 35°C. High humidity and regular rainfall support good growth. The plant needs full sunlight and frequent watering.

Orange

Orange grows well in well-drained sandy loam soil with a pH of 5.5 to 7.5. It prefers a warm climate with temperatures between 20°C and 30°C. Moderate humidity is suitable, and the plant requires full sunlight.

Strawberry

Strawberry grows best in well-drained sandy loam soil rich in organic matter. It prefers a cool climate with temperatures between 15°C and 25°C. Moderate humidity is ideal, and the plant needs full sunlight.

Papaya

Papaya grows well in well-drained sandy loam soil with a pH of 5.5 to 7.0. It requires a warm climate with temperatures between 25°C and 35°C. Moderate humidity is suitable.

Grapes

Grapes grow best in well-drained sandy loam soil with a pH of 6.5 to 7.5. They prefer a warm and dry climate with temperatures between 15°C and 35°C.

Blueberry
